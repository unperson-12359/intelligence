# Indelible

Public accountability platform tracking what public figures SAY vs what they DO.

## Skills

### /add-entity
Add a new public figure to the platform. Researches the figure, generates all data entries, edits mock-data.ts, downloads a photo, creates evidence assets, and verifies with a build.

Usage: `/add-entity <figure name>`

Skill file: `.claude/skills/add-entity/SKILL.md`
